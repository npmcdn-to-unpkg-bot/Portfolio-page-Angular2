Who am I?

I am a 29 year old student from Pori, Finland. I am studying Software Development in Satakunta University of Applied Sciences (SAMK). I am ambitious and I enjoy challenging projects and assignments at work and at home. What intrigued me the most at first was problem solving and the fact that you have to keep up to date with newest techniques and frameworks/libraries.

I used to be a Professional gamer, meaning I got paid to travel around Europe in different E-Sports tournaments playing Video Games. I did this for five years, while I was trying to get to know what I want to do in life and what is gonna be my REAL profession. For me Professional gaming was always just a hobby. Although it did get me paid, I never saw it as a true profession or something I would want to do for the rest of my life.

I learned a lot about PR and Marketing skills during the time, as being a professional gamer you have to interact with ones organization and its sponsors. My English got a lot better as the rest of my teammates were not from Finland and we communicated in English.

After starting my studies I've been keeping up with different new frameworks/libraries and technologies with solo projects on my free time. At the moment AngularJS 2.0, TypeScript 2.0 and ASP.NET WebApi interest me the most.
